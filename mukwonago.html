MUKWONAGO INDIANS




Head Coach

Mike Gnewuch

After the departure of coach Iverson in 2017, the torch was passed to Mike Gnewuch, or simply known as "Coach G" in Mukwonago. The former Fon Du Lac coach has turned the struggling Indians into a dominant force at the Division 1 level. The Indians had been running an old-school wishbone offense until Coach G's arrival. They still love to pound the rock, but it has a more modern look to it now. They finally made it back to the biggest stage in 2022, but a devastating early injury to their star running back saw them come up just short.


Mukwonago's Accomplishments (1991 - Present):

State Champions: 2004

Runner Up: 2022

State Qualifiers (Semi-final): 1998, 2013, 2021

Sectional Finals: 1993, 1999, 2005, 2014
