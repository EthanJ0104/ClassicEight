KETTLE MORAINE LASERS




Head Coach

Mathew McDonnell

A team on the rise. Kettle Moraine has been viewed as an average team for a long time now. Being in the Classic 8 doesn't help, living in the shadow of Mukwonago, Muskego, and Arrowhead. That status has come to an end, winning their first ever conference title this year, after capturing the state championship in 2022.
