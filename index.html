Classic 8 Football



Home to the best high school football in Wisconsin, the Classic 8 Conference has produced many state champions over the years. The conference consists of 8 schools from Waukesha county in southeastern Wisconsin.

This is the final season of its current teams. See the conference realignment page for information about the teams entering and exiting the conference.
