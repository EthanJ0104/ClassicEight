WELCOME: PEWAUKEE PIRATES




Head Coach

Justin Friske

The Classic 8 conference will have a new look for the 2024 season, with newcomers Pewaukee replacing Waukesha North and Waukesha South. With only 7 teams next year, each team will play a team from the Greater Metro conference to ensure everyone plays 9 games. This realignment aims to make games more competitive for Waukesha North and South. The Pirates won the division 3 state championship in 2021.


Pewaukee's state championship in 2021 is their only championship appearance in the WIAA era.
