WAUKESHA WEST WOLVERINES




Head Coach

Steve Rux

Not built until 1993, Waukesha West is the newest of three high schools in Waukesha. This split has seen Waukesha North and South fall off significantly in almost all sports. The Wolverines have had some amazing years, but have been no better than average the past several seasons. Head coach Steve Rux has put together many great teams during his years with the school.


Waukesha West's Accomplishments (1993 - Present):

State Champions: 2004, 2010

Runner Up: 2014, 2015

State Qualifiers (Semi-final): 2000, 2013, 2017, 2019

Sectional Finals: 2016, 2018
