OCONOMOWOC RACOONS




Head Coach

Sal Logue

Oconomowoc is still the new kid on the block. While this is soon to change, their arrival in 2017 still makes them the newest member of the Classic 8 Conference. The Racoons joining saw the "Classic 8" conference home to 9 teams up until Catholic Memorial's departure. Oconomowoc has never been a giant, so joining the Classic 8 has only made things more difficult for them. Simply qualifying for the playoffs seems to be a major accomplishment for them these days. However with a large community and a beautiful football field, only time will tell if they can become a stronger side.
