ARROWHEAD WARHAWKS




Head Coach

Matt Harris

There has always been one constant in Wisconsin high school sports: Arrowhead. The Warhawks seemingly dominate every sport, especially football. Being one of the most massive schools in the state certainly helps with this. Head coach Matt Harris took over in 2020 after the departure of Fritz Rauch. While he has not yet led Arrowhead back to the biggest stage, he's been right there each and every year.


Arrowhead's Accomplishments (1991 - Present):

State Champions: 1993, 1994, 1996, 2007, 2012, 2013

Runner Up: 2000, 2002, 2003, 2006, 2008, 2014, 2015

State Qualifiers (Semi-final): 2009

Sectional Finals: 1991, 1993, 2022
