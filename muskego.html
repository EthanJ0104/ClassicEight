MUSKEGO WARRIORS




Head Coach

Kenneth Krause

The rise of an empire. Muskego hasn't always been a powerhouse, but you wouldn't think that if you saw them now. It all started when the Warriors hired Ken Krause to be their new head coach. He lead Muskego to their first championship in 2018, and did it again in 2019. The Warriors had one of the longest active win streaks in the country at the time, more than 40 games. Their infamous Wing T offense and its trickery is something most teams struggle to defend.


Muskego's Accomplishments (1991 - Present):

State Champions: 2018, 2019

Runner Up: 1994

State Qualifiers (Semi-final): 2017

Sectional Finals: 2001, 2015, 2021, 2022
